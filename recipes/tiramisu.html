Tiramisu

This recipe makes a light and easy tiramisu for approximately 8 pax that can be adapted to be alcoholic or non-alcoholic based on your preference.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs with yolks and whites separated (Note: Make sure your crockery is dry as any water will interfere with its ability to form stiff peaks.)
  • 50g caster sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 250g mascaporne cheese
  • 5 tbsp kahlua, rum, Bailey's or other alcohol of your choice, or omit if making non-alcoholic version
  • At least 30 pieces of savoiardi
  • At least 180ml of espresso shots
  • Cocoa and sieve for dusting

Steps

  1. Beat yolks and sugar with an electric beater until the colour of the mixture turns from yellow to pale yellow and the consistency becomes thick. Add vanilla extract.
  2. Whisk egg whites in a clean and dry bowl till stiff peaks form.
  3. Add the cream mixture slowly and in parts to the beaten egg whites and gently fold through, taking care not to bash out all the air in the foam.
  4. Mix espresso shots and alcohol in a small and shallow dish. Dip the savoiardi and line the bottom of a dish of your choice. You can also use ramekins if you like, to make individual mini portions.
  5. Spread the mixture in Step 3 over this layer of savoiardi evenly. Top with another layer of coffee dipped savoiardi and cover with another layer of mixture from Step 3.
  6. Cover and refrigerate overnight, or at least 6 hours.
  7. Dust with cocoa powder just before serving.
